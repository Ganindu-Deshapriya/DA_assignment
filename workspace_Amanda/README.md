## 📊 Dataset Description

This dataset contains health survey data related to diabetes risk factors. Below is the description of each column:

- **Diabetes_012** *(category)* → Target variable:  
  - 0 = No diabetes  
  - 1 = Prediabetes  
  - 2 = Diabetes  

- **HighBP** *(int)* → High blood pressure (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **HighChol** *(int)* → High cholesterol (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **CholCheck** *(int)* → Cholesterol check in last 5 years (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **BMI** *(float/int)* → Body Mass Index  

- **Smoker** *(int)* → Smoked at least 100 cigarettes in life (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **Stroke** *(int)* → Ever had a stroke (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **HeartDiseaseorAttack** *(int)* → Coronary heart disease or myocardial infarction (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **PhysActivity** *(int)* → Physical activity in past 30 days (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **Fruits** *(int)* → Consumes fruit 1+ times per day (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **Veggies** *(int)* → Consumes vegetables 1+ times per day (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **HvyAlcoholConsump** *(int)* → Heavy alcohol consumption (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **AnyHealthcare** *(int)* → Has healthcare coverage (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **NoDocbcCost** *(int)* → Couldn’t see doctor due to cost (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  

- **GenHlth** *(category)* → General health rating:  
  - 1 = Excellent  
  - 2 = Very good  
  - 3 = Good  
  - 4 = Fair  
  - 5 = Poor  

- **MentHlth** *(float)* → Number of days in past 30 days mental health was not good (0–30)  
- **PhysHlth** *(float)* → Number of days in past 30 days physical health was not good (0–30)  
- **DiffWalk** *(int)* → Difficulty walking or climbing stairs (0 = No, 1 = Yes)  
- **Sex** *(int)* → Sex (0 = Female, 1 = Male)  

- **Age** *(category)* → Age group:  
  - 1 = 18–24  
  - 2 = 25–29  
  - 3 = 30–34  
  - 4 = 35–39  
  - 5 = 40–44  
  - 6 = 45–49  
  - 7 = 50–54  
  - 8 = 55–59  
  - 9 = 60–64  
  - 10 = 65–69  
  - 11 = 70–74  
  - 12 = 75–79  
  - 13 = 80+  

- **Education** *(category)* → Education level:  
  - 1 = Never attended school  
  - 2 = Elementary  
  - 3 = Some high school  
  - 4 = High school graduate  
  - 5 = Some college/technical school  
  - 6 = College graduate  

- **Income** *(category)* → Income level:  
  - 1 = < $10,000  
  - 2 = $10,000–$15,000  
  - 3 = $15,000–$20,000  
  - 4 = $20,000–$25,000  
  - 5 = $25,000–$35,000  
  - 6 = $35,000–$50,000  
  - 7 = $50,000–$75,000  
  - 8 = $75,000+
